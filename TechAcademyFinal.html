About

As a registered social worker, I believe healing happens in connection — to community, to purpose, and to the natural world.

I specialize in trauma-informed care, newcomer mental health, and community engagement. I’ve facilitated forest therapy walks, community garden gatherings, and youth leadership circles in parks and natural spaces.

When I’m not working, I’m hiking, journaling under the trees, or volunteering at environmental wellness programs. Nature is not just a hobby — it’s part of the therapy I offer.
